Overview

Bariatric and metabolic surgery, has grown rapidly in most western countries over the last two decades as a safe and effective treatment for patients with severe obesity and/or uncontrolled diabetes mellitus. This type of surgery is performed laparoscopically or robotically using 5-6 small key-holes, which are hardly visible. This department is backed by excellent infrastructure, state of the art labor room and operation theatres. The department of Bariatric provides care through various specialty sections such as:-

  • Bariatric (obesity) and metabolic surgery improves quality of life of the patient, improves survival, and is very cost effective.

  • This surgery has multiple benefits. Not only does it help individuals lose weight, it also improves a range of other medical conditions like diabetes.

  • Furthermore, it improves the confidence of the patient and improves work place prospects.
